Post-Nostalgia (2013)

Post-Nostalgia is the result of a year long investigation regarding the past interjecting itself into the present. There were four main components: a tongue-in-cheek identity system for the movement; design fortunes; and the history of graphic design which communicates the issue by comparing an artifact of design with it's modern day doppleganger.
